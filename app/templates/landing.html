FAQ

Common questions

Does it actually send emails?
Yes — via Brevo's infrastructure. They land in inbox, not spam. You can preview every email before it goes out.
Can I customize the email content?
You choose tone (polite, professional, firm) and language (Arabic, French, English). Custom templates come with Pro.
What happens when a client pays?
Mark the invoice as paid — all remaining reminders cancel instantly.
Is my data safe?
Private PostgreSQL database, HTTPS everywhere. We never share or sell client data.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. No contracts. One click from your billing page. Data stays on free plan forever.
Do my clients need to sign up?
No. They just receive a normal email. No account or app needed on their end.
