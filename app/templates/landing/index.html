FAQ

Common questions

Does it actually send emails, or just schedule them?
It actually sends them — via Brevo's email infrastructure, which means they land in inbox, not spam. You can preview exactly what your client will receive before it goes out.
Can I customize the email content?
You choose the tone (polite, professional, firm) and the language (Arabic, French, English). The reminders are pre-written and optimized to get responses. Custom templates are available on the Pro plan.
What happens when a client pays?
You mark the invoice as paid in InvoiceBot. Once marked paid, all remaining scheduled reminders are cancelled immediately — no awkward follow-ups after payment.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your data is stored in a private PostgreSQL database. We never share or sell client information. All connections are HTTPS encrypted.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. No contracts, no lock-ins. You can cancel from your billing page in one click. Your data stays accessible on the free plan with no expiry.
Do my clients need to sign up for anything?
No. Your clients just receive a normal email in their inbox. They need no account, no app, nothing. It works exactly like an email you sent yourself.
